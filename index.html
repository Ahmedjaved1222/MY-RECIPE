MY RECIPE OF SANDWICH

INGREDIENTS

  • EGGS
  • BREAD
  • TOMATOES
  • MAYO SAUSE
  • OIL

METHOD

    FIRSYLY TAKE A PAN AND HEAT IT UP FOR A COUPLE OF MINUTES.
  1. CUT THE SLICES OF BREAD IN TWO HALF SQUARE SHAPES
  2. BOIL THE EGGS AND THEM CUT IT INTO THIN PARTS
  3. CUT THE TOMATOES IN SMALL CIRCLE SLICES
  4. NOW MERIANTE THE EGGS TOMATOES IN THE BREAD SLICES
  5. NOW APPLY SOME MAYO SAUSE TO THE SANDWICH FOR BETTER TASTE YOU CAN
    USE SOME OF YOUR SPICES
  6. NOW GRILL THE SAMDWICH IN THE GRILL PAN
    7. NOW DECORATE THE SANDWICH INTO PLATE AND READY TO SERVE

NOW OUR YUMMY SANDWICH IS READY TO EAT
